Game Design Document: Asteroids

Concept: A minimalist, high-stakes space survival game where players pilot a ship through a hazardous asteroid field, managing momentum and splitting targets to survive.

Core Gameplay Mechanics
- Player Controls & PhysicsThrust: Applies forward acceleration in the direction the ship is facing.
- Rotation: Rotates the ship left or right using angular velocity.
- Drift: Space features zero friction. The ship maintains momentum until counter-thrust is applied.
- Fire: Shoots straight-line projectiles from the ship's nose.

Spatial Rules
- Toroidal Space (Screen Wrap): Moving past any screen edge instantly teleports the entity (ship, asteroid, or bullet) to the exact opposite side.
- Hyperspace Panic Button: Instantly teleports the ship to a random screen coordinate. If it teleports onto an asteroid or enemy ship or bullet, instant self-destruction upon arrival to balance its utility.

Game Entities & Behavior
- Asteroid Splitting System
    - Large Asteroids: Slowest speed. Splitting creates two Medium Asteroids.
    - Medium Asteroids: Medium speed. Splitting creates two Small Asteroids.
    - Small Asteroids: Fastest speed. Destroyed completely upon hit.
- Enemy Threats (Anti-Camping)
    - Large UFO: Spawns periodically. Shoots projectiles in random directions.
    - Small UFO: Spawns at higher scores. Actively targets and shoots toward the player's position.
    
Game Loop & Systems
- Scoring System
    - Large Asteroid: Lowest point value.
    - Medium Asteroid: Medium point value.
    - Small Asteroid: High point value.
    - UFOs: Highest point value (Small UFO yields more than Large UFO).
    
Progression & Flow
- Spawn: Player spawns in the center with 3 lives and temporary invulnerability.
- Clear Wave: Player must destroy all asteroids and UFOs on screen.
- Next Wave: Next level spawns a higher density of large asteroids at faster base speeds.
- Game Over: Triggers when all player lives hit zero.

Art Style
- Neon green line Art

Game Feel:
